# Executive Summary

## Objective

To analyze whether trader behavior and performance change based on Bitcoin Fear / Greed sentiment.

## Key Insights

1. Profitability varies across different sentiment conditions.

2. During Greed periods, traders often increase trade size which may increase both profit and risk.

3. Frequent traders create more volume, but they are not always the most profitable.

4. Some traders remain profitable consistently regardless of market mood.

## Trader Segments

- Frequent vs Infrequent Traders
- Profitable vs Loss-making Traders
- Large Trade Size vs Small Trade Size Traders

## Recommendations

1. Use smaller position sizes during Fear days.

2. Apply tighter stop loss during Greed days.

3. Avoid unnecessary overtrading during volatile sessions.

## Final Note

Market sentiment can be a useful signal when combined with discipline and proper risk management.
